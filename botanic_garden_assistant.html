Project pitch

Our mobile application is a garden assistant, which is primarily aimed at beginners who want to take care of a particular plant, but do not know where to start, and are also not devoted to the process of care and harvesting. In addition, our application will be a good tool for experienced gardeners. The main value is that it helps in plant care by providing various information on sowing, fertilization process, harvesting, the list of required seeds, thereby saving users time to find such information. Unlike many competitors, the application solves the problem associated with the complexity of caring for plants, taking into account the weather conditions, climatic and geographic characteristics of the selected area. The application will facilitate the process of leaving, because it will know if it rained in the area of ​​your site or not, if it got cold, and will tell you all this information! In addition, our assistant will select the optimal plants for your region based on all this information.
